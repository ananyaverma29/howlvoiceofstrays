HOWL: THE VOICE OF STRAYS

HOWL: The Voice Of Strays is a small venture started by Shivansh Prabhakar, Komal Verma and Deepika Joshi in the year 2019. The reson behind starting this NGO was to rescue, rehabiliate and provide shelter to the strays of Haldwani.
